Visit Costa Del Sol Beach

Where the sand is soft and the waves are always rolling. Located on the coast of sunny California, Costa Del Sol boasts a wide collection of restaurants, activities, and night life that is sure to make your visit a memorable one.

Places To Explore

BEACHSIDE FUN

Discover more than just a beach. Costa Del Sol is the heart and soul of the greater SoCal area. Boasting miles and miles of crystal clear shores, Costa Del Sol offers endless sun and a variety of beachside amenities including bike and canoe rentals, 24/7 lifeguard services, and bonfire pits right on the beach!

SIGHTS ON THE PIER

Come visit the Costa Del Sol pier where guests are welcome to enjoy a variety of carnival games, rides, and food stands! Enjoy a premium Costa Del Sol hotdog on a stick, or take a ride on our world famous Ferris Wheel attraction. At Costa Del Sol, the family-fun activities are endless.

GOOD EATS

Feeling hungry? Costa Del Sol is home to a wide selection of beachside dining. From fresh, locally sourced sea food to premium gourmet burgers and shakes, Costa Del Sol welcomes you to enjoy our world class menu options.

TREAT YOURSELF

Purchase a seashell necklace from one of our vendor carts, catch a show from one of our many street performers, or treat yourself to a Costa Del Sol T-shirt. Make your visit to our beach a memorable one with a keepsake souvenir!
